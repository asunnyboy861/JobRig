Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

JobRig is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Data You Enter: Client names, contact info, job details, line items, and photos you add to the app. This data is stored locally on your device.
  • Cloud Data (Pro Only): If you subscribe to Pro and enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account via CloudKit. We cannot access this data.
  • Subscription Data: Payment processing is handled by Apple via StoreKit. We receive only a confirmation of your subscription status — never your payment details.
  • Feedback Data: If you contact support, we collect the information you voluntarily provide (name, email, message).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the JobRig app
  • To sync your data across devices (Pro, with your consent)
  • To respond to support requests
  • We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: All data is stored on your device using iOS secure storage
  • CloudKit: Pro sync data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple's CloudKit infrastructure
  • No Third-Party Servers: We do not operate external servers that store your data

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apple StoreKit: Handles in-app purchases. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Apple CloudKit: Handles data sync. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI API: Used for AI quote suggestions (Pro feature). We send only job description text — never personal client data. Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy

5. Your Rights

  • Delete Your Data: Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Export Your Data: Pro users can export data as CSV/PDF
  • Opt Out of Sync: Disable iCloud sync in Settings at any time
  • Request Data Deletion: Contact us at iocompile67692@gmail.com

6. Children's Privacy

JobRig is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
