Getting Started

  • Create a Quote: Tap the "+" tab, enter job details, add line items, and share as PDF
  • Convert to Invoice: Open any quote and tap "Convert to Invoice" — line items copy automatically
  • Add Clients: Go to the Clients tab and tap + to add contact info
  • Track Payments: Update job status from Quote Sent → Invoice Sent → Paid

Subscription Management

JobRig offers Free, Monthly ($9.99/mo), Yearly ($69.99/yr), and Lifetime ($149.99) plans.

  • View/Cancel Subscription: iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → JobRig
  • Restore Purchases: Open JobRig → More → Settings → Restore Purchases
  • Free Trial: 7-day free trial available for Monthly and Yearly plans
  • Subscription auto-renews unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period
  • Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period

Free vs Pro

  • Free: 3 clients, 5 jobs/month, local storage, PDF sharing
  • Pro: Unlimited clients & jobs, CloudKit sync, AI suggestions, reminders, custom branding, data export

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does JobRig work offline? Yes! All core features work without internet. Cloud sync happens when you're back online.
  • How do I add my logo to PDFs? Go to More → Business Settings and fill in your business info. It appears on all generated PDFs.
  • Can I export my data? Pro users can export data as CSV or PDF from the Dashboard.
  • Is my data secure? All data is stored locally on your device. Pro users' sync data is encrypted via CloudKit.
