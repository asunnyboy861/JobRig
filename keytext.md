# App Store Metadata

## Promotional Text
Create professional quotes in 30 seconds, convert to invoices with one tap, and get paid faster. Works offline on the job site.

## Description
Stop losing jobs because you can't get a quote out fast enough. JobRig is the fastest quote-to-invoice app built specifically for contractors, freelancers, and service professionals who work in the field.

CREATE QUOTES IN 30 SECONDS
No more sitting at a desk after a long day. Open JobRig, type the job name, add line items, and share a professional PDF quote before you leave the job site. It really is that fast.

ONE-TAP INVOICE CONVERSION
Won the job? Open your quote and tap "Convert to Invoice." All line items, pricing, and client info copy over automatically. No re-typing, no mistakes.

WORKS OFFLINE
No cell signal? No problem. JobRig works fully offline. Create quotes, add clients, and generate PDFs without an internet connection. Everything syncs when you're back online.

PROFESSIONAL PDFs
Generate clean, branded PDFs with your business name, contact info, and payment details. Impress clients and get paid faster.

TRACK EVERY JOB
From Draft to Quote Sent to Invoice Sent to Paid. Track every job's status at a glance. See overdue invoices in red so nothing slips through the cracks.

SMART REMINDERS
Set follow-up reminders for quotes you haven't heard back on. Get notified when invoices are overdue. Never lose track of a payment again.

DASHBOARD INSIGHTS
See your total revenue, outstanding invoices, and monthly earnings at a glance. Know exactly where your business stands.

PHOTO DOCUMENTATION
Attach job site photos directly to any job. Great for before-and-after documentation and client communication.

AI QUOTE SUGGESTIONS (PRO)
Not sure how to price a job? Let AI help. Get smart pricing suggestions based on your job description. 50 suggestions per month included with Pro.

ICLOUD SYNC (PRO)
Sync all your data across iPhone and iPad seamlessly via iCloud. Start a quote on your phone, finish it on your iPad.
